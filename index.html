FAQ

Who are allowed to play our games?

Hyffe Games have started as an game company for everyone, and we still are that. Everybody with good skills to read and follow rules, are allowed to play our games!

Are there language requirements?

Basically yes, basically no. You are allowed to speak any language within rules. But in Discord server, you need to speak English.

How much am I required to pay for playing?

None! Our games are 95% free games. 5% comes from IAP, which stands for In-App-Purchases.

My payment did not go through. What do I do?

If your payment didn't go trough, you can be in touch to us straight up in email! We have payments only in BuildWorlds.. The email then is support@buildworldsgame.com
